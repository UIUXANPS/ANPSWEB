Empowering The Future Innovators: Integrating AI & Digital Literacy in K-12

As technology continues to reshape the world, the need to prepare our future leaders for the challenges and opportunities of an AI-powered society has never been more critical. This interactive workshop is designed for educators, administrators, and educational leaders who are committed to preparing students for the future. The workshop offers hands-on strategies, resources, and practical insights for integrating AI and digital literacy into your classroom or school curriculum. Through a combination of expert-led sessions, collaborative discussions, and real-world case studies, participants will explore the transformative potential of AI and its role in the education system.
