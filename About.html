About Us

The Association of National and Private Schools (ANPS) is a leading educational organization in Indonesia, committed to enhancing the quality of education across the nation. We bring together a diverse network of schools, educators, and professionals to foster collaboration, innovation, and excellence in teaching and learning.

We have a diverse group of member schools and individual members that come from SPK-schools and national schools as well. As an association, our core focus is to provide affordable and accessible professional development, and we do this through workshops, conferences, learning school tours, roadshows and ANPSmeets, which are networking sessions focused on non-academic teams, including Librarians, Systems and IT Administrators, Human Resources etc

How to be Our Partner?

Becoming an ANPS partner means being part of a collaborative network that’s shaping the future of education in Indonesia. Whether you’re a school, organization, or individual passionate about empowering learning, we welcome you to contribute to this meaningful journey. You can start it by looking through our proposal before joining us!
